Value is a very important thing in a society

According to the Bible which is the word of God: Virtue has been defined as the spiritual energy that the believer needs to reach the Celestial glory. So, glory and virtue are both fruits of God's calling. In 2 Peter 1:3, Is He not the One who has called us by glory and virtue.Grains of Peace's mission is to highlight these human values and, most importantly, to demonstrate that they can be applied in everyday life in light of the rise of violent behavior and criminal behavior. Along these lines, enduring human connections between individuals, including those of various religions and societies, can be fashioned.

What human values are they? The human qualities that are valuable to all of us, even to very young children, are known as human values. They are the virtues that direct us to consider other people when interacting with one another, thereby establishing a connection with other people by touching the humanity of the other from our own. These are the various positive attitudes that we value as individuals. They are the cornerstones of any socially viable existence: They make room for a dynamic, a move toward the other person, which brings peace.

It is through human values that it is possible to develop one's relationship with others, to work with others in cooperation, to dialogue together, and to resolve conflicts. These are our positive feelings based on our human feelings for the human essence of the other. The work of Grains of Peace aims to value human values as the foundation of psychosocial skills, the tool for managing human relationships, and the inspiration for peace.
